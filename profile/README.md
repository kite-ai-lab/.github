# 🪁 Kite AI Lab

> **Proof-based engineering and AI for world good.**

We build open tools and learning paths at the intersection of AI safety, engineering education, and real-world benefit.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Create engineers who understand deeply, build honestly, and help the world through technology.

## 🙏 Three Holy Commandments

Every artifact we create must pass three gates:

| Gate | Principle | Check |
|------|-----------|-------|
| 🔍 **Honesty** | No claim above evidence | Sources, boundaries, limitations |
| 🔨 **Creation** | Must teach or transfer skill | Tests, explanations, next steps |
| 🛡️ **Safety** | No external harm | Internal-only until human-approved |
